# 🌱 Greek & Latin Roots Practice App

A simple interactive vocabulary practice tool designed to help learners build confidence with Greek and Latin root words through repeated, low-pressure practice.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

This app supports vocabulary development by helping learners recognize patterns in academic words, especially words ending in **-ology**, meaning “study of.”

The goal is to reinforce root-word meaning through:

- Short practice rounds
- Immediate feedback
- Repeated exposure
- Clear root clues
- Low-pressure review

---

## 📚 Research Basis

This app was designed because research supports the value of teaching Greek and Latin roots as a way to strengthen vocabulary, reading comprehension, and academic language development.

This app was specifically designed to be friendly for **low-literacy learners, English learners, and learners with learning challenges or disabilities** by using clear prompts, repeated practice, immediate feedback, and visible root clues.

---

## 🧠 Learning Focus

This app helps users practice words such as:

- biology
- geology
- ecology
- zoology
- psychology
- anthropology
- chronology
- audiology

Each question includes a root clue and answer choices so learners can connect word parts to meaning.

## 💡 Why This Matters

Academic vocabulary can be difficult when students are expected to memorize whole words without understanding their parts.

This app supports pattern recognition by helping learners break words into meaningful roots.

For example:

> bio = life  
> -ology = study of  
> biology = study of life
